Programming Classwork 3

This programming assignment is where a program calculates the total score and the average score for the Quiz and classwork completed in this class. The quizzes and classwork categories are as follows: (1) Syllabus Quiz (2) Chapter 1 Quiz (3) Chapter 2 Quiz (4) Programming Classwork 1 (5) Programming Classwork 2. The user should enter the name and scores received ranging ranging from 0 to 100 for each grading item. The total score is the sum of all grading items and the average score is the average of all grading items.
